Hi there! I’m April Yang. I I started my journey in research by doing the work. Probing for “root cause” level of information and observing user behavior was crucial to the success of my work. I became obsessed with understanding users. Years of experience has fostered the intuition in me to dig deeper when came across insightful details, which adds values in understanding user behavior and uncovering insights.

I love data. I took the mixed-methods approach in our research work, which allowed us fully explore our research questions. I worked with metrics and data logs to interpret the stories behind the quantitative data. I believe in triangulating the data, all the while working around the constraints.

Being an effective communicator and being able to play well with others has helped me so much in my research career. I worked closely with design and engineering, knowing my audience has helped me engaging people effectively.

When I’m not working -
I help with the user research work at the non-profit organization Design x Us, who seeks to discover and share the stories behind the humans that power the creative industries.
My favorite Podcast is Mixed Methods by Aryel Cianflone. The book I’m currently reading is A Human Algorithm by Flynn Coleman.
I like playing piano. I’m not very good at it, I’m trying to get better.
I love coffee, producing videos, and spending time with my friends. 🍀
